Overview

MCS provides innovative IoT solutions to homes, industrial and mining sites, commercial and government buildings. MCS combines its electrical engineering knowledge and technology domain prowess to undertake turnkey project execution and proffer consulting services in the following areas;

  • PLC & SCADA Programming and Integration
  • Instrumentation and Process Control
  • Control and MCC Panel Building
  • CCTV surveillance and Access Control Systems
  • Traffic and Parking Management Systems
  • LT Electrical Engineering Systems
  • Domestic Electrical Wiring and Home Automation
  • Lighting and HVAC systems
  • Fire Suppression Systems
